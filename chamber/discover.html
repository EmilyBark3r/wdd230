Mountain Creek began as a humble logging town back in the early 1800's. In the year 1882 Buckley was officially founded

As of the 2020 census, the town has a population of 5,114 individuals.

Another one of Mountain Creek's defining features is its unrivaled view of Mt.Rainier

One of the staples of Mountain Creek is it's annual log show. Local residents and visitors from afar gather in large numbers to celebrate the event.

The Wally's drive-in has been a hallmark of Mountain Creek for nearly 60 years! Though the name and exterior have changed, the same small-town diner feel remains.

Mountain Creek is only a 15 minute drive from the beautiful Mount Rainier National Park. There, tourists may enjoy hundreds of hiking trails, fishing spots, and campsites.
